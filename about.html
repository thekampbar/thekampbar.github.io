The Kamp

About

The Kamp is a hidden gem located in downtown Harahan that opened in 2008.

We have live music on Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday nights.

The Kamp is also the perfect place to host your birthday party, company party, school reunion, or fundraiser.

We are open 7 days a week.
